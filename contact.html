More Info

As a club, we will be participating in HackClub's YSWS- You Ship, We Ship! At TechTogether, you will be guided in creating a project by experienced mentors. Then, the project is shipped to Hack Club in exchange for a variety of prizes, ranging from bubble tea to 3D Printers!

As a club member, you will be learning technology skills such as learning how to code in Python, HTML, and CSS, learning basic Computer Aided Design skills, as well as foundational hardware such as basic circuits and electronic components.

Our approach to teaching is project-based and collaborative- the main way one will learn is by creating a website, building a basic circuit, and communicating with their peers! We believe this fosters a collaborative mindset that is essential for growth.

Clubs and Events

Clubs

Meetings will be weekly, and mainly online. The date and time will be decided at a later date. The majority of meetings will take place online, on Zoom or a similar platform. However, we will be meeting in person occasionally to share projects and participate in hands-on activities! More information will be shared with club members upon their joining through the link.

Events

TechTogether aims to spread STEM learning through a variety of events, including hands-on activities, hackathons, and more! Club members will be able to attend, but they are open to everyone in the public. Specific events will be introduced and promoted when they are created, and will occur in-person. We aim for these events to be as inclusive as possible, so they are open to all skill levels! Keep updated on our Instagram and on the website for notices on upcoming TechTogether events.
